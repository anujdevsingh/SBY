# Sahyog Bima Yojna (SBY) - NGO Policy Summary

## About This Scheme

Sahyog Bima Yojna is an insurance and contribution scheme for members.

## Key Policies

### Membership
- Members must contribute monthly to remain active in the scheme
- New members can join by paying the required contribution amount
- Membership status is determined by regular contributions

### Insurance Coverage
- Insurance amount is calculated based on the number of members and contributions
- Members receive coverage for accidents and emergencies
- Coverage multiplies as more members join the scheme

### Contribution Rules
- Monthly contribution: ₹100 per month
- Insurance amount: 1000 times the contribution (₹100,000 for ₹100/month)
- Missing 4 or more months of contributions may result in losing insurance benefits
- Members can rejoin after missing payments

### Benefits
- Medical support through Sahyog funds
- Insurance payout is 100% for amounts up to ₹10,000
- Insurance payout is 50% for amounts above ₹10,000 (minimum ₹10,000)

### Future Expansion
- After one year, the scheme may expand to cover serious illnesses
- Critical illness coverage will be added based on scheme performance

### Membership Growth Benefits
- When you add 4-5 new members, your insurance amount doubles (200x)
- When 200 members join, insurance amount increases to 200x
- More members = higher insurance coverage for everyone

### Governance
- Elections held for working members (कार्यकारी सदस्य)
- Must be a member for at least 1 year to participate in elections
- Age requirement: Must be 55 years or older for certain positions
- Annual meeting on March 31st to determine formulas and policies

### Important Notes
- There is no mandatory repayment requirement for medical assistance
- Pension scheme may be available after certain conditions are met
- Members who stop contributing cannot participate in elections
